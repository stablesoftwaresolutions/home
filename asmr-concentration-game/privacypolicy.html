Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5th of November 2019

Stable Software Solutions respects your privacy. ASMR Concentration Game collects completely anonymous data that helps us develop better, more engaging games. We do not collect any personal information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily (such as contacting our customer support).

This privacy policy clarifies what information is collected, why it is collected, and how the information is handled by us. We may periodically update this privacy policy.

What information is collected

Usage Data

As you play our game, anonymous usage data is automatically collected. We collect this data to understand how our game is played and learn how to improve it. The data includes generic information, such as how often the game is played and which devices are used, as well as game- specific information, such as how many times level is completed.

Some of the generic usage data is provided to us by Apple.

You can opt-out of anonymous usage data collection at any time from your iOS device settings (Privacy > Analytics).

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Advertising data

We use third party advertising networks to provide advertising for our game. This allows us to provide ASMR Concentration Game free of charge. The advertising networks collect various anonymous statistics, such as how often certain ads are displayed and how often they are clicked. Google Admob Privacy Policy

Support requests

If you contact us for support (e.g. via email), your contact information will be stored in our support database only as long as is necessary to handle the particular support case. We may also request further information relevant to your issue, such as details about your device and operating system. We will not be able to identify you from the automatically collected usage data based on the information you have provided.

How the data is shared

We may share aggregated reports about the usage of our games with third parties or the public. Individual users are not identifiable from these reports.

We will never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent unless we are legally obliged to do so by law enforcement officials.

How the data is secured

We will take appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your data from misuse and unauthorized access.
